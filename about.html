About

FYMC is a fork of the percision web client, so that people can play minecraft on their browser n shit again

You may be wondering, "why"? Why not? Its not like this will circulate around the internet enough to get taken down, this is just a little thing between me, and friends. Or atleast, I hope it stays that way. There are folks out there so bored out of their minds they need something to keep them afloat. There are some people who can't even play minecraft on their device. The answer to this problem? FYMC.
